What is FIRE?

FIRE stands for Financial Independence, Retire Early. While you may or may not want to retire early, it can be helpful to know where you stand financially.
Ironically, by paying a little more attention to your finances, you may find that you can have freedom from your finances.

FIRE is a movement that encourages people to save and invest aggressively in order to achieve financial independence. Key components are living within your means, saving a large percentage of your income, and investing wisely. The goal is to have enough savings and investments to cover your living expenses without relying on a traditional job.
